 "EduPulse" - Student Dropout Risk Prediction for E-learning
🧑‍💼 Client Brief:
EduPulse is an edtech platform offering live courses. Many students don’t complete their courses. Management wants to predict and reduce dropout.

📌 Requirements:
Analyze user engagement (video views, quiz attempts, login time).

Define dropout (e.g., 50% video unviewed + no login in 14 days).

Create ML model to flag risk early.

Identify reasons (bad content? timing? workload?).

✅ Deliverables:
Dropout risk model.

Weekly cohort analysis.

Visual insights + heatmaps.

PDF report + model performance metrics.
