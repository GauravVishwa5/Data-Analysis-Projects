"FinTrack360" – Expense Categorization & Saving Pattern Analysis App
🧑‍💼 Client Brief:
A fintech startup wants to help users manage their finances by analyzing transaction data from SMS, bank statements, and UPI logs.

📌 Requirements:
Categorize expenses (food, travel, bills, shopping, etc.).

Detect recurring expenses (Netflix, EMI, rent).

Analyze income vs expense trends over months.

Predict monthly surplus or deficit.

✅ Deliverables:
Expense breakdown dashboard.

Savings trend chart.

PDF report with budget suggestions.

Predictive model for future expense alert.
