🧑‍💼 Client Brief:
CaféPulse is a growing café chain with 30+ outlets. They want to understand which products are top sellers, peak hours, customer preferences, and optimize inventory based on demand.

📌 Requirements:
Analyze transactional sales data (items, timestamp, outlet ID).

Track top-selling items by time/day/outlet.

Analyze customer frequency (loyalty behavior).

Suggest items to remove or combo deals to create.

Build an interactive dashboard (Power BI or Tableau preferred).
✅ Deliverables:
Sales heatmap by hour/day.

Top products by outlet.

Customer segmentation (new vs repeat).

Dashboard + 2-page strategy recommendation doc.
