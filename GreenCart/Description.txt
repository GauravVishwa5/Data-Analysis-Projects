"GreenCart" - Price Optimization for Online Grocery
🧑‍💼 Client Brief:
GreenCart is an online grocery platform struggling with pricing. They suspect some products are overpriced or underperforming.

📌 Requirements:
Analyze past 12 months' sales data (price, quantity sold, category).

Build a price elasticity model.

Identify products with low margin but high elasticity.

Recommend new price points for 20+ products.

✅ Deliverables:
Product-wise sales vs price correlation.

Price elasticity report.

Excel sheet with current & suggested prices.

Visualization of impact scenarios.
