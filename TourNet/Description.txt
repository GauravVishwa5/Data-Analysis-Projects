 "TourNet" - Travel Trend Analysis from Social Media
🧑‍💼 Client Brief:
TourNet is a travel agency that wants to launch tour packages based on what’s trending online (Insta, Twitter, blogs).

📌 Requirements:
Collect (scrape) posts with hashtags like #wanderlust, #travel2025.

Use NLP to extract most mentioned destinations.

Analyze seasonality and sentiment.

Identify influencers creating buzz.

✅ Deliverables:
Dashboard of trending destinations.

Sentiment chart of top 5 countries.

Word cloud + influencer mention report.

1-page launch plan for 2 tour packages.
