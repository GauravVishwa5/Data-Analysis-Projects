"CityEats" – Online Food Delivery Trends and Delay Analysis
🧑‍💼 Client Brief:
CityEats, a Zomato-like startup, wants to analyze their food order data to find delay causes and improve service time.

📌 Requirements:
Analyze delivery time vs estimated time.

Compare delays by area, weather, time of day.

Identify which restaurants cause delays.

Suggest traffic or kitchen improvements.

✅ Deliverables:
Delay heatmap by area and time.

Restaurant delay leaderboard.

Actionable insights for 10% faster delivery.

KPI dashboard with filters.
